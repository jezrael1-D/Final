About

Hinundayan is a coastal municipality in the province of Southern Leyte.
The municipality has land area of 59.90 square kilometers or 23.13 square miles
which constitutes 3.33% of Southern Leyte' total area.


Hinundayan is province that many people will remember for
it's peaceful environment and generous people.
